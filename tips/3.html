How does Samsung Pay work?

Samsung Pay uses proprietary Magnetic Secure Transmission (MST) and Near Field
Communication (NFC) to make contactless mobile payments. MST and NFC enables the Galaxy
Note8, Galaxy Note Fan Edition, Galaxy S8, Galaxy S8+, Galaxy S7 Edge, Galaxy S7, Galaxy Note5,
Galaxy S6 Edge+, Galaxy A7(2017), Galaxy A5(2017), Galaxy A7(2016), Galaxy A5(2016), Galaxy
A9 Pro, Gear S3 and Gear Sport to make secure transactions with nearly every payment terminal
in the world.
