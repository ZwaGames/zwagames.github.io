Project Vision

Although Minecraft can be a very competitive game, we want everyone to be able to play on our server without fear of whether they will be able to achieve the challenges or if they do not have enough skill, that's why we want to clarify several points.

We have created this project because most of the Play To Earn are browser games and they are difficult to last over time.

We want this to be a community project and when it is fully up and running we will publish the code and turn it into a DAO.

We will assign you a skill level that will be updated every week and you will play with users of a similar skill level.

If you are a competitive player and you want to win great prizes, dont worry because we will have tournaments and monthly leaderboards!
