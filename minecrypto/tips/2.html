What are Samsung Pay's main features?

Samsung Pay enables users to make in-store contactless payments using Galaxy Note8, Galaxy
Note Fan Edition, Galaxy S8, Galaxy S8+, Galaxy S7 Edge, Galaxy S7, Galaxy Note5, Galaxy S6
Edge+, Galaxy A7(2017), Galaxy A5(2017), Galaxy A7(2016), Galaxy A5(2016), Galaxy A9 Pro,
Gear S3 and Gear Sport. Samsung Pay’s main features are its proprietary MST technology and its
NFC payments capability which makes it the most accepted mobile payment solution.
*Gear Sport only supports NFC payment.
